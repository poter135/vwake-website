FAQ · Frequently Asked Questions

What you'll want to know before listing

Will my voice be used to train AI?

No. Without your written consent, Vwake will never train AI voice models on your recordings, never synthesize content you didn't personally record, and never extend usage beyond the contract scope. Your audio is only downloaded and played within the Vwake app per the contract terms. If Vwake develops new AI-related features in the future, we will obtain your consent and renegotiate revenue terms — your prior authorization will not silently extend.

Can I delist anytime? What happens after termination?

Yes. After contract termination, we remove your audio from our system and servers and provide a deletion certificate. Specific termination terms are shared with the contract.

How does revenue share work? When do I get paid?

Revenue share model — every fan purchase earns you a cut. Specific revenue split, pricing, and payout details (bank transfer / tax handling) are shared with the partnership contract. Monthly payouts.

Is this exclusive? Can I still sell on Booth?

Not exclusive. The traditional static voice packs you sell on Booth or Fanbox are different from Vwake's in-app alarm voice format. Vwake doesn't restrict you from continuing to sell traditional packs elsewhere — we only require app-internal usage rights for the 3 clips you record for us.

How many clips do I need to record? What's the format?

3 clips (3 moods × 1 each) performs best with fans. If recording conditions are limited, 1–2 clips is also fine — pricing and revenue share don't change.

Each clip is 10–15 seconds, recommended moods are gentle / energetic / tsundere, with wake-up greeting or "five more minutes" themes (content is freeform). The recording bar is low — your phone's built-in mic in a quiet room is enough. No professional studio needed.

My subscriber count is small. Can I still list?

Absolutely welcome! Vwake's mission has always been to bring together creators of all kinds. If you're interested in collaborating on voice content, that's wonderful news for us. We're excited to see more diverse voices joining the platform.

I represent an agency / management company. How do we partner with Vwake?

We welcome agency partnerships. Open the apply form via the "Join Vwake" button above and switch to the "Agency / Management" tab to send us your agency details — we'll work with you on a multi-talent deal covering contract terms, revenue share, and rollout schedule under a single agreement. You can also email us directly at contact@hocatech.com.
